Jeevamrutha (Liquid)

Primary Soil Microbial Stimulant

Ingredients for 1 Acre:

  • Water 200 L
  • Desi Cow Dung 10 kg
  • Desi Cow Urine 10 L
  • Jaggery (Black) 2 kg
  • Pulse Flour (Besan) 2 kg
  • Virgin Soil 1 Handful

ICAR/ZBNF Guidelines:

Stir clockwise twice daily. Ferment for 48-72 hours. Apply during irrigation. Max efficacy within 7 days of preparation.

Beejamrutha (Seed Treatment)

Fungal & Soil Disease Protection

  • Dung 5 kg
  • Urine 5 L
  • Lime (Chuna) 50 g
Use to coat seeds before sowing. Protects from root rot and seed-borne fungus. Essential for Dryland farming.

Ghanjeevamrit (Solid)

Dry Fermented Basal Dose

Preparation: Mix dung, urine, jaggery and flour into a thick paste. Dry in shade.

Calibrated Dose:

100 kg / 1 Acre

Shelf Life:

6 - 8 Months

Kunapajala (Ancient Manure)

Vrikshayurveda Protein-Rich Liquid

Core Components:

  • Animal/Fish waste (Flesh/Marrow)
  • Sesame oil cake & Honey
  • Black gram & Jaggery
TNAU Scientific Reference: Ferment for 30 days. Dilute 1:10. Excellent for rejuvenating old fruit orchards and stress recovery.

Dashparni Ark

Universal Bio-Pesticide (10 Leaves)

Ferment 10 medicinal bitter leaves in cow urine for 40 days to create a powerful insect repellent.

Usage Ratio 2.5% (2.5L in 100L Water)
Main Target Sucking Pests & Caterpillars

Soil Microbiology

Rhizosphere Dynamics

The area around the roots where microbes thrive. Jeevamrutha repopulates this zone with beneficial bacteria that fix nitrogen and solubilize phosphate.

Gokripa Amrit

A concentrated microbial culture. Once obtained, it can be multiplied indefinitely using jaggery and buttermilk to rapidly restore dead soil.

Personalized Recommendation

Tailored strategies based on your land type and resource availability.

Dryland (Arid)

Low Resources

Focus on Amrit Pani and thick straw mulching. Prioritize drought-hardy millets.

Full Resources

Use Beejamrutha for seeds. Apply Ghanjeevamrit at sowing. Setup wind-breaks using local trees.

Rainfed (Watershed)

Low Resources

Contour digging. Use Sanjivak (Quick fermentation) to boost soil during short monsoon spells.

Full Resources

Integrate Horticulture. Use liquid Jeevamrutha via trenching. Focus on moisture harvesting.

Irrigated (Wet)

Low Resources

Shift to Waphasa (Aeration) technique. Use FAA (Fish Amino) for nitrogen needs.

Full Resources

Multi-cropping system. Use Panchagavya foliar spray. High density Kunapajala application.
